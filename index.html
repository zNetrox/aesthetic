what is aesthetic rap?

Aesthetic Rap is a subgenre of hip hop that focuses on creating a unique and artistic sound. It is characterized by its use of abstract and experimental production techniques, often incorporating elements of jazz, soul, and ambient music. Artists in this genre often strive to create a unique and artistic sound that stands out from the mainstream.
